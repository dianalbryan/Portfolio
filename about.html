Who I am

Raised in Colorado, on a steady diet of literature and folklore, I found I enjoyed bridging gaps with others. I've studied literature and graphic design, but also have skill in web design and UX/UI. I use literature as a base to help me understand different people and their needs, but my strength is in making designs that provide the look and feel that clients want.

As a rule in design, I like to find new ways to explore ideas, blending new and traditional styles to make something new and beautiful. I also like to keep in touch with my clients. If, in a few years, there is is something new you want to try, or even a few changes you want to make, I am avaliable and always happy to help and touch up older projects.

Currently, I live in Cary, North Carolina with one very insistent kitten. In my spare enjoy hiking, coffee, tea, writing, illustrating and creating original content on my own.

I'm looking forwards to meeting you.
